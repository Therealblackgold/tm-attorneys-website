FEES

We are very cognizant of the fact that access to legal representation can be very costly. That is why we offer three structure depending on the needs of the client, the urgency and complexity of the matter and the nature of the matter.

Our primary fee offering is based on an hourly rate. However, considering various factors, we may offer the option of a lumpsum quote subject to payment of a deposit.

In exceptional cases where a client is unable to afford an hourly rate or lumpsum payment, we may offer to assist the client on a contingency fee basis subject to certain conditions and the provisions of the Contingencies Fees Act 66 of 1997

*all clients will be required to sign our mandatory terms of engagement and provide us with the necessary FICA documents.
